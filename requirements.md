# Requirements

## Registration

- Individual registration only
- $85 per player
- 68 player capacity
- Waitlist after capacity reached

## Payment

- Venmo only
- Screenshot upload required
- Admin manually verifies payment

## Teams

- 4-person best ball scramble
- Teams balanced manually
- Preferred player requests honored when possible

## Admin

- Verify payments
- Manage registrations
- Manage waitlist
- Create teams
- Export CSV

## Tech

- Next.js
- Neon Postgres
- Neon Auth
